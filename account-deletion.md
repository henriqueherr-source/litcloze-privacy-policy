# LitCloze — Account & Data Deletion

**App:** LitCloze (Learn Lithuanian)
**Developer:** ClozeAnki

LitCloze lets you permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data
at any time. There are two ways to do it.

## Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

1. Open **LitCloze**.
2. Tap your profile avatar (top of the Practice screen) to open the **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm. Your account and all of its cloud data are permanently deleted.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email **clozanki@gmail.com** from the address linked
to your account and ask us to delete your account. We will process the request and
confirm by email.

## What is deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your **account information** (email, name, and profile photo from Google Sign-In).
- Your **learning data** synced to the cloud (decks, cards, review history, settings,
  and progress).

This deletion is **immediate and permanent** — there is no additional retention
period for your personal account data, and it cannot be recovered.

## What is kept

- **Anonymous analytics:** a small number of non-identifying app-usage events (e.g.
  onboarding completed, session completed) are not linked to your identity and are
  not personally identifiable, so they are retained in aggregate.
- **On-device content:** bundled sentences, decks, and any local progress stay only
  on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app.
