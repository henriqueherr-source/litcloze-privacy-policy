# Privacy Policy
*Last Updated: 8 June 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how LitCloze ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and synchronisation services.

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide a better experience, synchronise your learning progress, and communicate updates.
* **Account Information:** When registering or signing in via Google Sign-In, we collect your email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
* **Learning Progress & Activity Logs:** We store learning state details (review counts, correct reviews, accuracy, day streaks, daily activity heatmap, and other progress statistics) to support cloud synchronization. We also store details of your cards, decks, and words (including custom/imported decks, card reviews, intervals, ease factors, repetitions, lapses, and optional user notes).
* **Consent Preferences:** We record your explicit preferences regarding promotional emails and newsletter lists.
* **Billing and Payment Processing:** For future premium paid features, payment transactions are handled exclusively and securely by Google Play Billing and Apple App Store Billing. We do not store, process, or view credit card details on our servers.

### 2. How We Use Information
* **Core Application Services:** To process review schedules, calculate spaced-repetition metrics, and back up your progress across devices.
* **Educational Updates & Marketing (Optional Opt-In):** With your active consent, we send newsletters, product updates, learning tips, and promotional offers. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link included in each email.
* **Service Improvement & Maintenance:** To keep the app working reliably and to improve the learning experience.

### 3. Data Storage & Sharing
Your data is securely stored on Google Cloud Firebase servers. We do not sell your personal data. We only share information under the following conditions:
* **Service Providers:** Hosting, authentication, and database services provided by Google/Firebase. For future premium features, payment processing is handled by Google Play Billing / Apple App Store Billing.
* **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud, or protect safety.

### 4. Advertising (Not Currently Active)
LitCloze does **not** currently display advertisements or collect advertising identifiers, and the app contains no third-party advertising SDKs.

We may introduce advertising in a future version to help support a free tier. **If and when we do**, we may work with mobile advertising networks that use advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID, Apple IDFA) and related device data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Before any such advertising or advertising-identifier collection begins, we will:
* update this Privacy Policy and our Google Play Data Safety disclosures to reflect the change; and
* where required by law, request your consent at that time.

Your continued use of the app after such an update will be subject to the revised policy. Until then, no advertising data is collected.

### 5. GDPR & Your Rights
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you possess specific data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
* **Right of Access and Rectification:** You can request details of stored data and correct any inaccuracies.
* **Right to Erasure (Data Deletion):** You can delete your account and all associated cloud backups permanently at any time directly in the app under the Profile tab ("Delete account" button), or by contacting support.
* **Right to Object/Withdraw Consent:** You can opt out of promotional communications at any time.
