# Privacy Policy
*Last Updated: June 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how LitCloze ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and synchronisation services.

### 1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide a better experience, synchronise your learning progress, and communicate updates.
* **Account Information:** When registering or signing in via Google Sign-In, we collect your email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
* **Learning Progress & Activity Logs:** We store learning state details (XP, day streaks, current level, total reviews, correct reviews, daily activity heatmap, and review statistics) to support cloud synchronization. We also store details of your cards, decks, and words (including custom/imported decks, card reviews, intervals, ease factors, repetitions, lapses, and optional user notes).
* **Consent Preferences:** We record your explicit preferences regarding promotional emails and newsletter lists.
* **Device Telemetry & Future Advertising Identifiers:** The app is currently ad-free. However, to support future free tiers, we may partner with mobile advertising networks that collect advertising identifiers (e.g., Google ADID, iOS IDFA) and general telemetry to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
* **Billing and Payment Processing:** For future premium paid features, payment transactions are handled exclusively and securely by Google Play Billing and Apple App Store Billing. We do not store, process, or view credit card details on our servers.

### 2. How We Use Information
* **Core Application Services:** To process review schedules, calculate spaced-repetition metrics, and backup progress across devices.
* **Educational Updates & Marketing (Optional Opt-In):** With your active consent, we send newsletters, product updates, learning tips, and promotional offers. You can unsubscribe from these at any time via the link included in each email.
* **Monetization and Maintenance:** In the future, we may serve advertisements or premium features. We will use device identifiers and subscription status to deliver these services.

### 3. Data Storage & Sharing
Your data is securely stored on Google Cloud Firebase servers. We do not sell your personal data. We only share information under the following conditions:
* **Service Providers:** Hosting, authentication, and database services provided by Google/Firebase. In the future, payment processors (Google Play/Apple Billing) and verified advertising partner platforms.
* **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud, or protect safety.

### 4. GDPR & Your Rights
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you possess specific data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
* **Right of Access and Rectification:** You can request details of stored data and correct any inaccuracies.
* **Right to Erasure (Data Deletion):** You can delete your account and all associated cloud backups permanently at any time directly in the app under the Profile tab ("Delete account" button), or by contacting support.
* **Right to Object/Withdraw Consent:** You can opt-out of promotional communications at any time.

### 5. Developer Contact Details
For questions, support, or data access requests, please contact:

* **Company Name:** ClozeAnki
